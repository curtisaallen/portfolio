ABOUT ME

Hello!

I'm Curtis Allen, a Senior Web Developer based in Atlanta, GA, with over 19 years of professional experience in Web Development. My academic background is in interactive development with a minor in design. Throughout my career, I've collaborated with both large and small companies, specializing in front-end development. I've also worked on WordPress theme development and freelanced on the side. Additionally, I have completed several training programs, including "Developing in Salesforce Commerce Cloud Training," "Umbraco Certified Professional Level 1 certification," and courses from the EMORY Center for Lifelong Learning. I have a strong interest in JavaScript and have undergone training in React, Vue.js, and Angular.

I've worked extensively with various web technologies and have received professional training in web usability, ASP.net 3.5, SQL, and Ruby on Rails. My work has been featured in Web Designer Magazine, and I've been honored with an ADDY Award for my contributions to PayWareMobile.com.

Outside of Coding

When I'm not coding, I enjoy spending quality time with my wife and four sons. I also indulge in reading tech books and watching video training to stay updated with the latest industry trends.
